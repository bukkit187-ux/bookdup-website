FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about BookdUp.

Q. How does it work?

BookdUp answers your business line 24/7 — entirely by text. Clients text to book, and it confirms the appointment, sends reminders, and fills your slow days automatically. You set it up once and just show up and work.

Q. Do clients need an app?

No. Clients just send a regular text message — no app, no download, no login, no account. It works on any phone.

Q. Does it work in Spanish?

Yes. BookdUp automatically detects when a client is writing in Spanish and replies in Spanish — and switches back to English just as easily. Nothing to configure.

Q. How do I see my schedule?

Text schedule (or agenda) any time and you'll get today's bookings right back. You also get a text the moment a new booking comes in, so you always know your day in context.

Q. What if a client needs to cancel or reschedule?

They simply reply can't make it (or no puedo ir) and BookdUp takes care of it — opening the slot back up. No awkward phone calls or missed messages.

Q. How much does it cost?

Simple, flat monthly pricing — no per-booking fees, no setup cost:

Tier 1 $19.99/mo Tier 2 $34.99/mo

Tier 1 covers booking, reminders, and schedule by text. Tier 2 adds deposit collection when clients book.

Q. What if I'm running late?

Text delay 15 (or tarde 15) and BookdUp lets the affected clients know you're running about 15 minutes behind, with their updated time. Text ready (or listo) when you're back on schedule.

Q. Is client information secure?

Yes. Client details are encrypted and never sold or shared. Your dashboard is private and password-protected, so only you can see your bookings.

Q. How do I get started?

Tap Get Started anywhere on the site to open the quick sign-up form. Setup takes about an hour — we build everything for you, then your booking line goes live.

Q. How do I get help?

Already a BookdUp customer and need a hand? Email our support team at bookduphq@gmail.com and we'll get back to you. (To sign up as a new customer, tap Get Started instead.)
